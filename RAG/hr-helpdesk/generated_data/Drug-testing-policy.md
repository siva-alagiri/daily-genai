## Acme Corp Email Usage Policy

### Policy Brief and Purpose

This Acme Corp Email Usage Policy outlines the expectations and guidelines for the appropriate and secure use of the company’s electronic mail system. As a leading Information Technology firm, email is a fundamental communication channel, and its responsible use is critical to maintaining our operational efficiency, protecting sensitive data, and upholding our professional reputation. This policy aims to ensure that all email communications align with Acme Corp’s values, legal obligations, and security standards.

### Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and any third-party individuals or entities granted access to Acme Corp’s email systems, accounts, and associated services, regardless of their employment status or location. This includes individual and departmental email addresses.

### Policy Elements

Company email is a vital communication asset provided to facilitate business operations and collaboration. While we offer a degree of flexibility for limited personal use, the primary purpose of company email is for professional activities.

#### Appropriate Use of Company Email

Employees are authorized to use their Acme Corp email for all legitimate business-related purposes, including but not limited to:

*   Communicating with clients, partners, vendors, and colleagues.
*   Accessing and utilizing company-approved software and online platforms necessary for job functions.
*   Participating in professional development, industry forums, and networking events that benefit Acme Corp.
*   Receiving official company communications and updates.

#### Inappropriate Use of Company Email

When using Acme Corp email, employees represent the company. Therefore, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

*   Sending or forwarding content that is discriminatory, harassing, offensive, or defamatory.
*   Registering for or subscribing to services that are unauthorized, illegal, or pose a security risk to Acme Corp.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam) to internal or external recipients.
*   Disclosing confidential, proprietary, or sensitive company, client, or employee information without explicit authorization.
*   Engaging in political lobbying, personal commercial activities, or any unlawful conduct.
*   Creating or transmitting chain letters, hoaxes, or other disruptive content.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor all email communications on its systems to ensure compliance with this policy and for security purposes.

#### Limited Personal Use

Employees are permitted to use their company email for limited personal communications, provided such use:

*   Does not interfere with job performance or productivity.
*   Does not involve the disclosure of confidential company information.
*   Adheres to all other aspects of this policy, including security and appropriate use guidelines.
*   Is not excessive or for personal gain.

Examples include registering for professional development courses, receiving non-work-related notifications, or brief communications with family and friends.

#### Email Security

Email is a common vector for cyber threats. Employees must exercise vigilance to protect Acme Corp’s systems and data.

*   **Password Management:** Set strong, unique passwords (minimum 12 characters, combining uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols) and change them every 90 days. Never share passwords or write them down in an unsecured location.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:**
    *   Do not open suspicious attachments or click on unverified links.
    *   Be wary of emails requesting personal or login information, even if they appear to be from internal sources.
    *   Verify the sender’s identity and email address for any unusual or unexpected messages.
    *   Report any suspected phishing attempts or malware to the IT Security team immediately.
*   **Data Handling:** Avoid sending sensitive or confidential information via unencrypted email. Use approved secure file transfer methods when necessary.
*   **Device Security:** Ensure all devices used to access company email are protected with up-to-date antivirus software and operating system patches.

#### Email Signature

Employees are encouraged to use a professional email signature that reflects Acme Corp’s branding guidelines. This typically includes:

*   Employee’s Full Name
*   Job Title
*   Department
*   Acme Corp (with official company link)
*   Official Company Phone Number
*   Company Address

Images, company logos, and links to official company social media profiles may be included, adhering to brand guidelines. For assistance with signature setup, contact the IT Helpdesk or your direct supervisor.

### Disciplinary Consequences

Violations of this Email Usage Policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include, but is not limited to, verbal warnings, written reprimands, suspension, and termination of employment. Severe breaches, particularly those involving data security, legal non-compliance, or significant reputational damage, may result in immediate termination and potential legal action. Each incident will be investigated thoroughly and handled on a case-by-case basis.
