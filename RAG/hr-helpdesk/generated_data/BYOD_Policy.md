## Work-From-Home Policy

**Effective Date:** [Current Date]

### 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Work-From-Home (WFH) Policy is to establish clear guidelines and expectations for eligible employees of Acme Corp who perform their duties remotely. This policy aims to foster a flexible work environment that supports employee well-being, enhances productivity, and ensures business continuity while maintaining the highest standards of data security and operational efficiency for our Information Technology services.

### 2. Eligibility

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp. Eligibility for remote work arrangements will be determined by the nature of the role, job responsibilities, and the employee's performance history. Roles requiring constant physical presence, specialized hardware, or direct client interaction that cannot be replicated remotely may be excluded.

### 3. Approval Process

Employees interested in a WFH arrangement must submit a formal request through the designated HR portal to their immediate supervisor. Requests will be evaluated based on business needs, team dynamics, individual performance metrics, and the suitability of the role for remote execution. Final approval rests with the department head and HR.

### 4. Equipment and Security

4.1. Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining the security and confidentiality of all Acme Corp information and assets. This includes adherence to the company's Cybersecurity and Data Protection policies.
4.2. Acme Corp will provide essential equipment, such as laptops and necessary software licenses, for remote work. Employees are expected to use company-issued equipment for all work-related tasks.
4.3. Employees must ensure a secure, private internet connection and a dedicated workspace free from distractions. Acme Corp is not responsible for personal internet service costs.

### 5. Work Hours

5.1. Remote employees are expected to adhere to their agreed-upon work hours, ensuring availability for team collaboration and client engagements. Any deviations from standard working hours must be pre-approved by the supervisor.
5.2. Overtime and time-off policies, as outlined in the Employee Overtime Policy and Paid Time Off Policy, remain applicable to remote employees.

### 6. Communication

6.1. Remote employees must maintain proactive and regular communication with their supervisors and team members using company-approved collaboration tools (e.g., Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zoom).
6.2. Prompt responses to emails, messages, and meeting invitations are expected to ensure seamless team operations and client service.

### 7. Productivity and Performance

7.1. Performance expectations for remote employees are consistent with those for in-office employees. Performance will be measured by deliverables, quality of work, and contribution to team and company objectives.
7.2. Regular performance reviews and feedback sessions will be conducted to ensure accountability and continuous improvement.

### 8. Health and Safety

8.1. Employees are responsible for establishing and maintaining a safe and ergonomically sound work environment at their remote location.
8.2. Any work-related injuries or incidents occurring during WFH must be reported immediately to the supervisor and HR, following the Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Policy.

### 9. Expenses

Employees will be reimbursed for approved business expenses directly related to their remote work, such as specific software subscriptions or necessary office supplies, in accordance with the Employee Business Expense Policy. Personal utility costs (e.g., electricity, internet) are generally not reimbursable.

### 10. Termination of Remote Work Agreement

Acme Corp reserves the right to modify or terminate a remote work arrangement at any time, with reasonable notice, based on business needs, changes in job responsibilities, or an employee's performance.

### 11. Confidentiality

Remote employees must strictly adhere to all confidentiality and data protection policies. Unauthorized disclosure or misuse of sensitive company, client, or employee information will result in severe disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment and legal proceedings.

### 12. Compliance

Remote employees must comply with all applicable Indian laws and regulations, as well as all Acme Corp policies and procedures, while working remotely.

### 13. Acknowledgment

By participating in the WFH program, employees acknowledge their understanding and agreement to comply with the terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

### 14. Review and Updates

This policy will be reviewed periodically, at least annually, and updated as needed to reflect changes in business requirements, technology, or legal frameworks. Employees will be informed of any revisions through official communication channels.

### 15. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this Work-From-Home policy, please contact the Human Resources Department.
