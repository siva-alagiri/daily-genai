## Acme Corp Email Usage Policy

### POLICY BRIEF AND PURPOSE

This Email Usage Policy at Acme Corp establishes clear guidelines for the appropriate and secure use of company email accounts and systems. As a leading Information Technology company with a large workforce, email is a critical communication and collaboration tool. This policy aims to ensure professional conduct, protect sensitive company and client data, maintain system integrity, and uphold Acme Corp's reputation in the digital landscape.

### SCOPE

This policy applies to all Acme Corp personnel, including full-time employees, part-time employees, contractors, consultants, and any third parties granted access to Acme Corp's email infrastructure or who use company email addresses for business purposes. This includes email accessed via company-issued devices or personal devices.

### COMPONENTS OF THE POLICY

Company email accounts are provided for business-related communications and activities. While limited personal use is permitted, all users must prioritize professional conduct and data security.

#### Correct Use of Corporate Email

Employees are authorized to use their Acme Corp email accounts for activities that support the company's business objectives, including but not limited to:

*   **Professional Communication:** Facilitating official correspondence with clients, partners, vendors, and internal teams.
*   **Project Collaboration:** Sharing project-related documents, updates, and discussions.
*   **Accessing Business Resources:** Logging into approved software, platforms, and online services necessary for job functions.
*   **Networking:** Exchanging contact information at industry events or conferences for legitimate business development.
*   **Learning & Development:** Subscribing to industry newsletters, webinars, or educational content relevant to professional growth.

#### Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email

When using an Acme Corp email account, employees represent the company. Therefore, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

*   **Unlawful or Unethical Content:** Sending, receiving, or storing content that is illegal, discriminatory, harassing, defamatory, or offensive.
*   **Unauthorized Disclosure:** Sharing confidential, proprietary, or sensitive company, client, or partner information without explicit authorization.
*   **Spamming:** Sending unsolicited commercial emails or mass communications unrelated to legitimate business.
*   **Misrepresentation:** Impersonating others or falsely representing one's identity or affiliation with Acme Corp.
*   **System Abuse:** Intentionally introducing malware, viruses, or engaging in activities that could compromise the security or functionality of Acme Corp's systems.
*   **Competitive Activities:** Registering for or engaging with services or entities that are direct competitors of Acme Corp, unless explicitly authorized for business intelligence purposes.
*   **Excessive Personal Use:** Engaging in personal email activities that significantly interfere with work duties, consume excessive network resources, or violate other company policies.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor email communications on its systems in accordance with applicable laws and internal policies to ensure compliance and security.

#### Personal Use

Limited and reasonable personal use of company email is permitted, provided it:

*   Does not interfere with an employee's job performance or productivity.
*   Does not violate any company policies, including those related to data security, confidentiality, and code of conduct.
*   Does not involve illegal, unethical, or inappropriate content.
*   Does not incur significant costs or liabilities for Acme Corp.
*   Examples include brief communications with family or friends, or registration for personal development courses.

#### Email Security

Email is a primary vector for cyber threats. All employees must adhere to the following security protocols:

*   **Strong Passwords:** Create and maintain complex, unique passwords for email accounts, combining uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
*   **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Enable and utilize MFA for email access where provided.
*   **Phishing Awareness:** Exercise extreme caution with suspicious emails. Do not click on unfamiliar links, download attachments from unknown senders, or provide credentials in response to unsolicited requests.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Activity:** Immediately report any suspected phishing attempts, unauthorized access, or security incidents to the IT Security team.
*   **Data Handling:** Avoid sending sensitive or confidential information via unencrypted email. Utilize approved secure file transfer methods for such data.
*   **Device Security:** Ensure all devices used to access company email are protected with up-to-date antivirus software, firewalls, and operating system patches.

#### Email Signature

To maintain a consistent and professional brand image, all external emails sent from an Acme Corp account must include a standardized email signature. Employees should consult the Marketing or IT department for the approved signature format, which typically includes:

*   Employee's Full Name
*   Job Title
*   Acme Corp (with company website link)
*   Official Phone Number
*   Company Address
*   Optional: Company logo, relevant certifications, or social media links as approved.

### DISCIPLINARY CONSEQUENCES

Any violation of this Email Usage Policy will be thoroughly investigated and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, in accordance with Acme Corp's Disciplinary Policy. Severe breaches, such as unauthorized disclosure of confidential data, engaging in illegal activities, or causing significant security incidents, may also lead to legal action.
