# Work-From-Home Policy

**Effective Date:** [Current Date]

**1. Purpose:**
The Work-From-Home (WFH) Policy at Acme Corp is designed to provide a flexible work arrangement that supports employee well-being and productivity, while ensuring business continuity and operational efficiency. This policy outlines the framework, expectations, and responsibilities for employees who are authorized to perform their duties remotely.

**2. Eligibility:**
This policy applies to all permanent employees of Acme Corp. Eligibility for remote work is determined based on the nature of the role, job responsibilities, and the employee's consistent performance.
Factors considered for eligibility include:
*   Role suitability for remote execution.
*   Demonstrated ability to work independently and maintain productivity.
*   Availability of a suitable home workspace and reliable internet connectivity.
*   Consistent achievement of performance metrics and adherence to company policies.
*   Approval from the immediate manager and department head.

**3. Approval Process:**
Employees interested in a WFH arrangement must submit a formal request to their immediate supervisor, outlining the proposed schedule and rationale.
*   Requests will be evaluated based on business requirements, team dynamics, and individual performance history.
*   The supervisor, in consultation with the department head and Human Resources, will review and approve or deny the request.
*   Approved WFH arrangements will be documented and may be subject to periodic review or modification.

**4. Equipment and Security:**
*   Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptop, software licenses) to facilitate effective remote work. Employees are responsible for the proper care and maintenance of company-issued assets.
*   Employees must ensure the security and confidentiality of all company data and intellectual property when working remotely. This includes using secure networks, password-protecting devices, and adhering to Acme Corp's Cybersecurity and Data Protection policies.
*   Unauthorized access to company systems or data, or the use of personal devices for sensitive company work without explicit IT approval, is strictly prohibited.

**5. Work Hours:**
*   Remote employees are expected to maintain their regular work hours and be available for communication and collaboration during these times, unless alternative arrangements are formally approved.
*   Flexibility in daily start and end times may be permitted with prior manager approval, provided it does not impact team collaboration or client commitments.
*   All applicable Indian labor laws regarding working hours, breaks, and overtime will be strictly adhered to. Employees must accurately record their working hours as per company guidelines.

**6. Productivity and Performance:**
*   Performance expectations for remote employees are consistent with those for in-office employees. Managers will set clear objectives and performance indicators.
*   Regular performance reviews and feedback sessions will be conducted to ensure accountability and support continuous productivity.
*   Employees are expected to proactively manage their time and workload to meet deadlines and contribute effectively to team goals.

**7. Health and Safety:**
*   Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe, ergonomic, and distraction-free work environment at their remote location.
*   Acme Corp encourages employees to take regular breaks to prevent strain and fatigue.
*   Employees must report any work-related injuries or health concerns occurring during WFH hours to their supervisor and HR immediately.

**8. Expenses:**
*   Acme Corp will reimburse approved business expenses directly related to remote work, such as internet connectivity or utility allowances, as per the company's Employee Business Expense Policy.
*   Employees must submit expense reports with valid receipts for reimbursement.

**9. Termination of Remote Work Agreement:**
*   Acme Corp reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate any WFH arrangement at any time, based on business needs, changes in job responsibilities, or performance concerns, with appropriate notice.
*   Employees may also request to return to full-time office work, subject to business needs and available space.

**10. Confidentiality:**
*   Remote employees must strictly adhere to all confidentiality and data protection policies of Acme Corp.
*   Unauthorized disclosure, sharing, or access to sensitive company, client, or employee information is a serious breach and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment and legal proceedings.

**11. Compliance:**
*   Remote employees must comply with all applicable Indian laws, regulations, and all Acme Corp policies and procedures while working remotely.
*   This includes, but is not limited to, policies on Code of Conduct, Cybersecurity, and Data Privacy.

**12. Acknowledgment:**
By participating in the WFH program, employees acknowledge their understanding and agreement to comply with the terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

**13. Review and Updates:**
This policy will be reviewed periodically and updated as needed to ensure its effectiveness and alignment with evolving business practices and legal requirements. Employees will be informed of any changes.

**14. Contact Information:**
For any questions or concerns regarding this Work-From-Home policy, please contact the Human Resources Department at [HR Contact Email/Number].
