## Company Email Usage Policy

**POLICY BRIEF AND PURPOSE**

This policy outlines the guidelines for the appropriate and responsible use of corporate email accounts and systems provided by Acme Corp. As a leading Information Technology company with a global presence, email is a critical communication tool and a repository of valuable information. The purpose of this policy is to ensure that all email communications uphold Acme Corp's professional standards, protect its intellectual property, safeguard sensitive client and company data, and comply with all applicable laws and regulations in India and internationally. Adherence to this policy is essential for maintaining our reputation, operational integrity, and a secure digital environment.

**SCOPE**

This policy applies to all individuals granted access to Acme Corp's email systems, including permanent employees, contract staff, temporary workers, consultants, and any third parties utilizing Acme Corp email accounts or infrastructure, regardless of their location or device used for access.

**COMPONENTS OF THE POLICY**

Acme Corp's email system is provided primarily for business-related communications. While reasonable personal use is permitted, all users must prioritize business objectives and adhere to the principles outlined below.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees are authorized to use their Acme Corp email accounts for all legitimate business purposes, including but not limited to:

*   Official communication with clients, partners, vendors, and internal teams.
*   Collaboration on projects, sharing work-related documents, and coordinating tasks.
*   Receiving official company announcements, updates, and policy information.
*   Accessing and registering for business-critical applications, platforms, and services.
*   Professional networking and engagement at industry events, conferences, or webinars that benefit Acme Corp.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

When using Acme Corp email, employees represent the company. Therefore, they must not engage in any activities that could harm Acme Corp's reputation, compromise its security, or violate legal or ethical standards. Prohibited uses include:

*   Sending or forwarding content that is discriminatory, harassing, offensive, defamatory, or sexually explicit.
*   Disclosing confidential, proprietary, or sensitive company or client information to unauthorized individuals or entities.
*   Engaging in any form of unlawful activity, including fraud, phishing, or distribution of malware.
*   Registering for or accessing websites, services, or applications that are unreliable, illegal, or pose a security risk.
*   Sending unsolicited commercial emails (spam) or engaging in mass mailings not approved for business purposes.
*   Using email for personal financial gain, political lobbying, or any external business ventures without explicit company authorization.
*   Impersonating another individual or misrepresenting one's authority.
*   Circulating chain letters, hoaxes, or other non-business-related mass communications.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor all email communications and system usage in accordance with legal provisions and internal policies to ensure compliance and protect company assets.

**Personal Use**

Acme Corp acknowledges that employees may occasionally need to use their corporate email for limited personal communications. Such use is permitted under the following conditions:

*   Personal use must be minimal, infrequent, and not interfere with job duties or productivity.
*   It must not involve the disclosure of any confidential company or client information.
*   It must not violate any aspect of this policy or any other Acme Corp policy, including those related to harassment, ethics, or data security.
*   Employees should have no expectation of privacy for personal communications conducted via company email.

**Email Security**

Email remains a primary vector for cyber threats. All employees are responsible for maintaining the security of their email accounts and protecting Acme Corp's digital assets. Users must:

*   Create and maintain strong, unique passwords for their email accounts, adhering to Acme Corp's password policy (e.g., minimum length, complexity, regular changes).
*   Enable and utilize Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) where required.
*   Be vigilant against phishing attempts, suspicious links, and unsolicited attachments. Always verify the sender and content before clicking or downloading.
*   Report any suspicious emails, security incidents, or unauthorized access attempts immediately to the IT Security team.
*   Ensure that any devices used to access company email are protected with up-to-date antivirus software and security patches.
*   Avoid sharing email credentials with anyone, including colleagues or IT support, unless explicitly instructed by the IT Security team through verified channels.
*   Encrypt sensitive data in emails or attachments as per Acme Corp's data protection guidelines.

**Email Signature**

To maintain a consistent and professional corporate image, all outgoing emails from Acme Corp accounts must include a standardized email signature.

*   **Required Elements:** Full Name, Official Job Title, Department, Acme Corp, Company Website, Official Contact Number.
*   **Optional Elements:** Company Logo (as per brand guidelines), relevant certifications.
*   **Prohibited Elements:** Personal quotes, unauthorized images, links to personal social media profiles, or any content not approved by Acme Corp's Brand or Marketing teams.

Employees unsure about the correct signature format should consult their manager or the Marketing department for guidance and approved templates.

**DISCIPLINARY CONSEQUENCES**

Any violation of this Email Usage Policy will be taken seriously and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, in accordance with Acme Corp's Disciplinary Policy. Examples of violations that may lead to severe consequences include:

*   Unauthorized disclosure of confidential company or client data.
*   Engaging in harassment, discrimination, or other unlawful activities via email.
*   Repeated or intentional disregard for email security protocols, leading to security breaches.
*   Using company email for significant personal gain or competitive activities.
*   Any activity that causes substantial reputational damage or financial loss to Acme Corp.

Acme Corp reserves the right to investigate all suspected policy violations and take appropriate action based on the severity and frequency of the offense.
