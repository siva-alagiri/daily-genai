**Work-From-Home Policy**

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**1. Purpose**
The purpose of this Work-From-Home (WFH) Policy is to establish clear guidelines and expectations for employees of Acme Corp who are approved to work remotely. This policy aims to support a flexible work environment that promotes employee well-being, maintains high levels of productivity, ensures business continuity, and upholds the highest standards of data security and compliance.

**2. Eligibility**
This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp whose roles have been identified by their department head and HR as suitable for remote work. Eligibility is determined based on job function, performance history, and the ability to maintain productivity and collaboration from a non-office location. Employees on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) or those requiring close supervision may not be eligible.

**3. Approval Process**
Employees interested in a WFH arrangement must submit a formal request to their immediate supervisor, outlining the proposed schedule and rationale. All requests require approval from the department head and Human Resources, considering business needs, team dynamics, and individual performance metrics. Approved WFH arrangements will be documented and reviewed periodically.

**4. Equipment and Security**
4.1 Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining the security and confidentiality of all Acme Corp information and assets. This includes safeguarding company-issued equipment, ensuring secure network access (e.g., via VPN), and adhering to all cybersecurity protocols.
4.2 Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptop, monitor) and software licenses required for remote work. Employees are responsible for ensuring a stable and secure internet connection at their remote location. Any additional equipment requests must be pre-approved by IT and the department head.

**5. Work Hours**
5.1 Remote employees are expected to adhere to their agreed-upon working hours, ensuring availability for meetings and collaboration with their teams. Any deviation from standard work hours must be pre-approved by their supervisor.
5.2 All applicable labor laws regarding working hours, breaks, and overtime in India will be strictly followed. Non-exempt employees must accurately record all hours worked, including any overtime, through the designated time-tracking system.

**6. Communication**
6.1 Remote employees must maintain regular and proactive communication with their supervisors and team members. This includes responding promptly to emails, messages, and calls during working hours.
6.2 Employees are required to utilize company-approved communication and collaboration tools (e.g., Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zoom) to ensure effective teamwork and information sharing.

**7. Productivity and Performance**
7.1 Performance expectations for remote employees are consistent with those for in-office employees. Managers will set clear objectives and monitor performance to ensure accountability and productivity.
7.2 Regular performance reviews and feedback sessions will be conducted to support remote employees in achieving their goals and addressing any challenges related to their work arrangement.

**8. Health and Safety**
8.1 Employees are responsible for establishing and maintaining a safe, ergonomic, and distraction-free work environment at their remote location. Acme Corp encourages employees to follow best practices for health and safety while working from home.
8.2 Acme Corp is not liable for personal injuries or accidents that occur at an employee's remote workspace, unless directly attributable to company-provided equipment or specific work-related instructions.

**9. Expenses**
Employees will be reimbursed for pre-approved, legitimate business expenses directly related to their remote work, in accordance with Acme Corp's Employee Business Expense Policy. This typically includes approved software, specific office supplies, or pre-authorized internet/utility subsidies, but excludes general household expenses.

**10. Termination of Remote Work Agreement**
Acme Corp reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate any remote work arrangement at its sole discretion, with appropriate notice, based on business needs, changes in job responsibilities, performance concerns, or policy violations.

**11. Confidentiality**
Remote employees must strictly adhere to Acme Corp's Confidentiality and Data Protection policies. Unauthorized disclosure of sensitive company, client, or employee information, regardless of intent, may result in severe disciplinary action, up to and including termination and legal proceedings.

**12. Compliance**
All remote employees must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies, including those related to data privacy (e.g., India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act), information security, and ethical conduct, while working remotely.

**13. Acknowledgment**
By participating in the WFH program, employees acknowledge their understanding and agreement to comply with all terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

**14. Review and Updates**
This policy will be reviewed periodically, at least annually, or as needed, to ensure its continued effectiveness, relevance, and compliance with evolving business requirements and legal standards. Employees will be informed of any changes through official communication channels.

**15. Contact Information**
For any questions or concerns regarding this Work-From-Home Policy, employees should contact the Human Resources department.
