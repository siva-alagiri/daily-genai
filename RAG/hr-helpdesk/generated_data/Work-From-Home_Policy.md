## Company Email Usage Policy

**POLICY BRIEF AND PURPOSE**

This policy outlines the principles for the appropriate use of corporate email accounts by employees of Acme Corp. Its purpose is to ensure secure communication, safeguard proprietary information, maintain professional conduct, and protect the company's digital assets and reputation.

**SCOPE**

This policy applies to all individuals granted access to Acme Corp's email systems, including permanent employees, temporary staff, contractors, and consultants. It covers all email accounts issued by Acme Corp, whether individual or departmental.

**COMPONENTS OF THE POLICY**

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Acme Corp email accounts are primarily for business-related communications and activities. Permitted uses include, but are not limited to:
- Official correspondence with clients, partners, and colleagues.
- Accessing company-approved software and services.
- Professional networking at industry events.
- Subscribing to relevant professional development resources.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

Employees must not use corporate email for activities that could harm Acme Corp's reputation, expose it to legal risks, or compromise its security. Prohibited uses include:
- Transmitting offensive, discriminatory, or harassing content.
- Engaging in unauthorized competitive activities.
- Sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam).
- Accessing or registering for illegal, fraudulent, or inappropriate online services.
- Distributing unauthorized marketing or solicitation materials.

**Personal Use**

Limited and reasonable personal use of corporate email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job responsibilities, violate company policies, or compromise security. Examples include:
- Registration for professional development courses.
- Brief personal communications with family or friends.
- Downloading safe, appropriate content for personal growth, ensuring no confidential data is shared.

**Email Security**

Email security is paramount to protect Acme Corp from cyber threats and data breaches. Employees are required to:
- Use strong, unique passwords (minimum 12 characters, mix of cases, numbers, symbols) and update them every 90 days.
- Exercise caution with suspicious emails, attachments, and links.
- Verify sender legitimacy and look for unusual grammar or formatting.
- Report any suspected phishing attempts or security concerns to the IT Security team immediately.
- Ensure all company-issued devices have up-to-date security software.
Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor email usage for security and compliance purposes.

**Email Signature**

All official corporate emails must include a standardized signature block to maintain a consistent professional image. This typically includes:
- Employee's Full Name
- Job Title
- Department
- Acme Corp (with company website link)
- Official Contact Number
Employees may include the company logo. Any additional content, such as personal quotes or external links, requires prior approval from the Marketing or HR department. Guidelines for signature creation are available from the IT Helpdesk.

**DISCIPLINARY CONSEQUENCES**

Non-compliance with this Email Usage Policy will result in disciplinary action, which may include verbal warnings, written reprimands, suspension, or termination of employment, depending on the severity and frequency of the violation. Serious breaches, such as unauthorized disclosure of confidential information, transmission of illegal content, or actions causing significant harm to Acme Corp, may lead to immediate termination and potential legal action.
