**Work-From-Home Policy**

**Effective Date:** [Current Date]

**1. Purpose**
This Work-From-Home (WFH) Policy at Acme Corp outlines the framework for flexible work arrangements, enabling eligible employees to perform their duties remotely. Our aim is to foster a productive, adaptable, and employee-centric work environment that supports work-life integration while maintaining operational excellence and data security. This policy ensures clarity on expectations, responsibilities, and procedures for remote work.

**2. Eligibility**
This policy applies to all permanent full-time employees of Acme Corp. Eligibility for remote work is determined based on the nature of the role, its suitability for remote execution, the employee's performance record, and team requirements. Roles requiring constant physical presence, specialized equipment, or direct client interaction on-site may not be eligible. Employees must have successfully completed their probationary period to be considered.

**3. Approval Process**
Employees interested in a WFH arrangement must submit a formal request to their direct manager, outlining the proposed schedule and rationale.
*   **Manager Review:** The manager will assess the request based on team needs, project timelines, and the employee's performance.
*   **HR Approval:** Approved requests from managers will be forwarded to the Human Resources department for final review and approval, ensuring consistency and compliance with company policy and Indian labor laws.
*   **WFH Agreement:** Upon approval, a formal WFH agreement will be signed by the employee, manager, and HR, detailing the terms, duration, and expectations of the arrangement.

**4. Equipment and Security**
*   **Company-Provided Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary IT equipment (e.g., laptop, software licenses) required for remote work. Employees are responsible for the proper care and maintenance of this equipment.
*   **Secure Environment:** Employees must ensure their remote workspace is secure, protecting company data and intellectual property. This includes using secure networks, password-protecting devices, and preventing unauthorized access to company information.
*   **Data Protection:** Adherence to Acme Corp's Data Protection and Cybersecurity policies is mandatory at all times, regardless of work location.

**5. Work Hours**
Remote employees are expected to maintain their regular working hours as agreed upon with their manager, ensuring availability during core business hours for collaboration and meetings. Any deviation from standard hours, including overtime or time-off requests, must be pre-approved by the manager and comply with Acme Corp's Overtime and Leave policies and applicable Indian labor regulations.

**6. Communication**
Effective and proactive communication is crucial for successful remote work.
*   **Regular Engagement:** Remote employees must maintain regular communication with their managers and team members through company-approved tools (e.g., Microsoft Teams, Slack, email, video conferencing).
*   **Responsiveness:** Employees are expected to be responsive to communications during their agreed-upon work hours.
*   **Meeting Participation:** Active participation in virtual team meetings and discussions is required.

**7. Productivity and Performance**
Performance expectations for remote employees are consistent with those for in-office employees. Managers will conduct regular performance reviews and provide feedback to ensure accountability and productivity. Performance metrics and objectives will be clearly defined and mutually agreed upon.

**8. Health and Safety**
Employees are responsible for establishing and maintaining a safe, ergonomic, and distraction-free work environment at their remote location. Acme Corp will provide guidelines on ergonomic setup. Employees must report any work-related injuries or incidents occurring in their remote workspace to their manager and HR immediately. Acme Corp is not liable for non-work-related incidents or injuries at the employee's remote location.

**9. Expenses**
Acme Corp will reimburse employees for approved business expenses directly related to remote work, such as specific software or essential office supplies, in accordance with the company's Employee Business Expense Policy. Personal utility costs (e.g., internet, electricity) are generally not reimbursed unless explicitly stated in the WFH agreement or required by law.

**10. Termination of Remote Work Agreement**
Acme Corp reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate any remote work arrangement at any time, with or without notice, based on business needs, changes in job responsibilities, or performance concerns. Employees will be given reasonable notice where practicable.

**11. Confidentiality**
Remote employees must strictly adhere to all confidentiality and data protection policies of Acme Corp. Unauthorized disclosure, access, or misuse of sensitive company, client, or employee information will result in severe disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment and legal proceedings.

**12. Compliance**
All remote employees must comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations in India, as well as all Acme Corp policies and procedures, while working remotely.

**13. Acknowledgment**
By participating in the Work-From-Home program, employees acknowledge their understanding and agreement to comply with all terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

**14. Review and Updates**
This policy will be reviewed periodically, at least annually, or as needed, to ensure its effectiveness, relevance, and compliance with evolving business needs and legal requirements. Employees will be informed of any changes through official communication channels.
