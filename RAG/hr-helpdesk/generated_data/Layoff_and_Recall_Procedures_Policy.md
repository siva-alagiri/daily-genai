**Work-From-Home Policy**

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**1. Purpose**

This Work-From-Home (WFH) Policy at Acme Corp establishes a framework for remote work arrangements, promoting flexibility, enhancing productivity, and ensuring business continuity while upholding our commitment to data security and employee well-being. It outlines the expectations and responsibilities for employees operating outside traditional office premises.

**2. Eligibility**

Remote work eligibility at Acme Corp is determined by the nature of the role, job responsibilities, and individual performance. This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees whose roles are deemed suitable for remote execution by their department head and Human Resources. Employees must have successfully completed their probationary period and maintain a satisfactory performance record.

**3. Approval Process**

Employees interested in a WFH arrangement must submit a formal request through the designated HR portal to their immediate supervisor. Requests will be evaluated based on business needs, team dynamics, job requirements, and the employee's performance history. Final approval rests with the department head and Human Resources.

**4. Equipment and Security**

4.1 Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining the security and confidentiality of Acme Corp's information assets. This includes adhering to all data protection, cybersecurity, and privacy policies.
4.2 Acme Corp will provide necessary company-issued equipment (e.g., laptops, software licenses) and secure network access (e.g., VPN) required for remote work. Employees are responsible for ensuring a stable internet connection at their remote location.

**5. Work Hours**

5.1 Remote employees are expected to adhere to their regular work hours as defined by their employment contract and team schedules, unless alternative arrangements are formally approved. Core working hours for collaboration will be communicated by respective teams.
5.2 Overtime and time-off policies, in accordance with applicable Indian labor laws and company guidelines, remain fully applicable to remote employees. All working hours must be accurately recorded.

**6. Communication**

6.1 Remote employees must maintain consistent and proactive communication with their supervisors and team members. This includes regular check-ins, participation in virtual meetings, and timely responses to communications.
6.2 Acme Corp-approved communication and collaboration tools (e.g., Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zoom) must be utilized for all work-related interactions.

**7. Productivity and Performance**

7.1 Performance expectations for remote employees are consistent with those for in-office employees. Performance will be measured by deliverables, outcomes, and adherence to deadlines.
7.2 Regular performance reviews and feedback sessions will be conducted to ensure accountability, support professional development, and address any performance-related concerns.

**8. Health and Safety**

8.1 Employees are responsible for establishing and maintaining a safe, ergonomic, and distraction-free work environment at their remote location.
8.2 Acme Corp is not liable for injuries or incidents that occur at the employee's remote workspace that are unrelated to work activities or company-provided equipment. Employees must report any work-related injuries or hazards immediately.

**9. Expenses**

Employees will be reimbursed for pre-approved, legitimate business expenses directly related to remote work, in accordance with Acme Corp's Employee Business Expense Policy. This excludes personal utility costs or general home office setup expenses unless explicitly agreed upon.

**10. Termination of Remote Work Agreement**

Acme Corp reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate any remote work arrangement at any time, with or without cause, based on business needs, performance, or changes in policy. Employees will be given reasonable notice where practicable.

**11. Confidentiality**

Remote employees must strictly adhere to all confidentiality and data protection policies. Unauthorized disclosure, access, or misuse of sensitive company, client, or employee information, regardless of location, will result in severe disciplinary action, up to and including termination and legal proceedings.

**12. Compliance**

Remote employees must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and Acme Corp policies while working remotely, including those pertaining to working hours, data privacy, and ethical conduct in India.

**13. Acknowledgment**

By participating in the WFH program, employees acknowledge their understanding and agreement to comply with all terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

**14. Review and Updates**

This policy will be reviewed periodically, at least annually, and updated as needed to reflect changes in business operations, technology, and legal requirements. Employees will be informed of any revisions through official communication channels.

**15. Contact Information**

For any questions or concerns regarding this Work-From-Home Policy, please contact the Human Resources Department at hr@acmecorp.com.
