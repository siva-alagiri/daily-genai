## Company Email Usage Policy

### POLICY BRIEF AND PURPOSE

This policy outlines the guidelines for the appropriate use of Acme Corp's email system. As a critical communication channel, email reflects our professional image and is vital for business operations. Its purpose is to ensure secure, ethical, and efficient communication, safeguarding company data, intellectual property, and reputation.

### SCOPE

This policy applies to all Acme Corp employees, contractors, consultants, and any third parties granted access to or using Acme Corp's email systems, whether individual or departmental accounts.

### COMPONENTS OF THE POLICY

Acme Corp's email system is provided primarily for business-related communications. While limited personal use is permitted, all users must prioritize professional conduct and adhere to the guidelines below.

#### Correct Use of Corporate Email

Users are authorized to utilize their Acme Corp email for all legitimate business activities, including but not limited to:
*   Communicating with clients, partners, and colleagues.
*   Accessing business-critical applications and services.
*   Professional networking and information gathering relevant to their role.
*   Receiving work-related updates and industry news.

#### Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email

Users must refrain from using Acme Corp email for activities that could compromise the company's integrity, security, or reputation. Prohibited uses include:
*   Sending or forwarding offensive, discriminatory, or harassing content.
*   Engaging in unauthorized commercial activities or solicitations.
*   Registering for unapproved or suspicious third-party services.
*   Distributing spam, chain letters, or unsolicited mass emails.
*   Disclosing confidential or proprietary company information without authorization.
*   Any activity that violates company policy or applicable laws.
*   Users acknowledge that Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor email usage for compliance and security purposes.

#### Personal Use

Limited personal use of company email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with work duties, consume excessive resources, or violate any other aspect of this policy. Examples of acceptable personal use include:
*   Brief communications with family or friends.
*   Registration for professional development courses or relevant community events.
*   Accessing non-work-related content that is appropriate and does not pose a security risk.

#### Email Security

Protecting Acme Corp's email system from threats like malware, phishing, and data breaches is a shared responsibility. Users must:
*   Maintain strong, unique passwords for their email accounts, adhering to IT's password policy (e.g., minimum length, complexity, regular changes).
*   Exercise caution with suspicious emails, attachments, and links. Verify sender identity before interacting with unexpected content.
*   Report any suspected phishing attempts or security incidents immediately to the IT Security team.
*   Ensure all company-issued devices used for email access have up-to-date security software and operating system patches.

#### Email Signature

All outgoing emails should include a standardized professional signature that reflects Acme Corp's brand identity. Users must:
*   Include their full name, job title, and Acme Corp's official name and website.
*   Adhere to the company's branding guidelines for logos or other visual elements.
*   Avoid including personal quotes, images, or links that are not business-related or approved by Marketing/HR.
*   Contact the Marketing department for guidance on official signature templates.

### DISCIPLINARY CONSEQUENCES

Failure to comply with this Email Usage Policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, depending on the severity and frequency of the violation. Examples of serious violations include:
*   Unauthorized disclosure of sensitive or confidential company information.
*   Sending or receiving emails containing illegal, harassing, or highly inappropriate content.
*   Repeated disregard for security protocols, leading to system vulnerabilities.

### Disclaimer

This policy serves as a general guide and should not be considered a legal document. Acme Corp reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. Employees are encouraged to seek clarification from HR regarding any aspect of this policy.
